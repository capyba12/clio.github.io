About Us

About Us

We’re creating a vibrant collection of 4,444 adorable, doodle-style capybaras, ready to make waves on Cardano!


At our core, we’re a team of capybara enthusiasts on a mission to build a fun and welcoming community that shares our love for these gentle giants. While our artwork is designed to be top-notch, our true focus is creating the best ways to reward and engage our holders through thoughtful rewards, engaging experiences, and more while keeping the entry barrier low.


Capybaras aren’t just the world’s largest rodents—they’re also some of the chillest, most lovable creatures around. Their peaceful nature and social bonds inspire everything we do.


Whether you’re here for the art, the community, rewards or just some good capybara vibes, there’s a warm spot waiting for you in the Crew.

Let’s build something special - one cozy capybara at a time.
